Research snapshot

What I work on

Three threads run through my research: sustainable materials, data-driven design, and energy.

Composites · ML

Machine learning for hybrid composites

Predicting the tensile properties of HDPE/CaCO₃/palm-kernel-shell composites with classical ML models — turning agricultural waste into engineered material, guided by data.

Energy storage

Battery materials

High-throughput screening of solid electrolytes for sodium-ion batteries, and porous-electrode modeling plus solvothermal synthesis of LiMn₂O₄ cathodes for tricycle batteries.

Catalysis · Deep learning

CO₂-to-fuels catalysis

Crystal graph convolutional neural networks and Monte Carlo simulation to screen porous materials (MOFs) for photoelectrochemical CO₂ reduction.
